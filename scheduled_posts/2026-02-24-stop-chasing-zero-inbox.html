Stop Chasing Zero Inbox

A mindset shift from “empty inbox” to “reliable response.”

Zero inbox feels good, but it can become a distraction. The real goal is a reliable response system.

Define two or three check‑in times per day. Respond or schedule, then close the inbox.

If something requires real work, put it on your task list and block time for it.

  • Set fixed check‑in windows.
  • Convert messages into tasks.
  • Protect focus time in between.
