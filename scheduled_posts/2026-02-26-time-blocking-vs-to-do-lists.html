Time Blocking vs. To‑Do Lists

A straightforward comparison of two popular productivity methods.

To‑do lists are great for capture. Time blocking is great for execution.

Lists answer “what.” Blocks answer “when.” Most people need both: list everything, then block the important work.

If you struggle with finishing tasks, try moving your top three into the calendar.

  • Lists: capture everything.
  • Blocks: execute the important work.
  • Use both for balance.
