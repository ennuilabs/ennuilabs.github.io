The Weekly Review in 20 Minutes

A short, repeatable review that keeps your week aligned without a long ritual.

A weekly review doesn’t need to be heavy. It just needs to reset your priorities and clean up loose ends.

Spend 5 minutes clearing your inboxes. Spend 10 minutes reviewing ongoing projects. Spend 5 minutes picking next week’s top outcomes.

Keep a single page of priorities so you start Monday with clarity.

  • 5 minutes: clear inboxes.
  • 10 minutes: review projects.
  • 5 minutes: choose next week’s outcomes.
