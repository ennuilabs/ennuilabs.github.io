Lightweight Calendar Tools Worth Considering

A short, accessible overview of calendar tool categories and why each exists.

Most calendars do the basics well. The difference is in how you access them and how they integrate with your day.

Web calendars are universal. Desktop apps are fast. Mobile apps are best for capture on the go.

Pick one primary calendar and keep others read‑only to avoid conflicts.

  • Choose one primary calendar.
  • Keep secondary calendars read‑only.
  • Prefer the tool you will actually open.
